My experience is in development and manufacturing of electronic components. After obtaining my PhD in Chemical Engineering from the University of California, Berkeley in Chemical Engineering (with an Electrochemical Engineering focus), I worked for several years as a researcher on electrochemical sensor, batteries, and fuel cells. I then worked for several years in the field of EMI shielding developing metal-filled gaskets and paints. Since 1996 I've been working on development of passive components such as capacitors, inductors and resistors. A signficant part of this work has been with capacitors that have one electrode that is an intrisincally conducting polymer.
